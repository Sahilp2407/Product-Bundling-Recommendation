Product Bundling — Business Report

Bundling is the hero. Everything else supports conversion, trust, and retention.

Author: Sahil Pandey • Oct 2025

Executive Summary

  • On-page bundling raises AOV and cuts decision friction.
  • Clean checkout with UPI QR; addresses and orders saved to Firebase.
  • Curated + graph-based recos on PDP; totals update live.

1) Product Bundling Strategy

  • Objective: Increase AOV via relevant accessories.
  • Logic: Curated map + compatibility graph (one-hop).
  • UX: Bundle modal on PDP; instant totals in checkout.
  • Data: Address before payment; order on place-order.
Pricing
  • Visible savings/strike-through.
  • Optional tiers: Starter (1), Pro (2).
  • Margin guardrails on bundle discount.
KPIs
  • Attach rate • Take rate
  • AOV uplift • Return delta

2) Marketing Concepts (Applied)

  • Customer-centric: remove friction, show "works well together".
  • Value = convenience + savings + compatibility assurance.
  • 4Ps/7Ps mapped to PDP merchandising and checkout messages.

3) Customer Lifecycle & Retention

  • Awareness → PDP → Bundle add → Checkout → Address save → Order → Revisit.
  • Activation: Bundle modal + QR pay. Retention: saved address/orders.
  • Measure: Conversion, AOV, repeat rate, cohorts.

4) Branding & Relationship Building

  • Brand promise: Smart, convenient, good-value tech shopping.
  • Trust: Transparent pricing, ratings, clean QR payment.
  • Relationship: Less friction on repeat via saved data.

5) Sales Strategy

  • PDP-first selling; helpful add-ons at the exact decision moment.
  • Anchoring with MRP vs bundle price.
  • Choice architecture: pre-select one high-fit accessory, keep options few.

6) Customer Retention Models

  • RFM (planned): Recency, Frequency, Monetary → VIP/At-risk segments.
  • NPS (planned): Track Promoters − Detractors trend.
  • CLV (simple): ARPU × margin × time horizon.

7) Kano Model

  • Must-be: Smooth checkout, accurate totals, address persistence.
  • Performance: Speed (QR, prefilled forms).
  • Delighters: Smart, relevant bundle suggestions.

8) Sales Motivation & Team Performance

  • Targets: Attach rate %, take rate %, AOV uplift.
  • Incentives: Reward quality bundles, control returns.
  • Enablement: Simple scripts + a small live KPI dashboard (planned).

9) Data & Systems

  • Auth: Anonymous sign-in → guaranteed uid.
  • Firestore: users/{uid}/addresses, users/{uid}/orders + mirror orders/*.
  • Realtime DB: users/{uid}/addresses (parallel write).
  • DSA: Graph + Set for recos; dictionary lookups; reduce for totals.

10) Risks & Mitigations

  • Margin erosion → discount guardrails.
  • Irrelevant recos → tune curated rules/edges.
  • Bundle returns → clear policy + recalculation.

11) Roadmap (Short)

  • Top‑K ranking (max‑heap) for best add‑ons.
  • Threshold discount: Pick any 2 → 7% off.
  • Mini dashboard: AOV, orders/day, attach rate.

Conclusion

  • Bundling is the primary growth lever.
  • Lean implementation today; extensible for analytics and ranking.
  • Clear path to higher revenue and better UX with minimal risk.

Sources

  • Marketing frameworks: 4Ps/7Ps, Lifecycle, RFM, NPS, CLV, Kano.
  • Choice architecture and pricing psychology best practices.
